Goblin Empire Economy + Cross-Pollination Design

Core rule: no skill exists in a silo. Every resource should feed a recipe, quest, unlock, gear upgrade, region prep loop, or combat loop.

This HTML is a readable companion to the spreadsheet. The spreadsheet is the source of truth for filtering, formulas, GP values, and balancing.

CookingSurvival conversion skillFish, meat, crops, herbs, logs/rangesHealing food, buffs, resist mealsCombat, Exploration, Bossing, Dangerous gatheringFood should be a real resource sink, not just vendor trash.
SmithingGear and infrastructure productionOre, bars, coal, logs, monster partsTools, weapons, armour, arrowheads, nailsAll gathering, Combat, Ranged, Region unlocksSmithing should unlock faster gathering and world shortcuts.
CraftingCross-skill glueHides, bones, logs, gems, silk, herbs, barsBows, charms, bags, jewelry, light armourRanged, Defence, Exploration, Utility, QuestsMonster drops become useful through crafting.
AttackWeapon accuracy/tierWeapons, food, potionsCombat access, monster dropsDrops, quests, region safetyAttack unlocks weapon classes, not raw progress alone.
StrengthDamage scalingWeapons, buff foodFaster kills, boss viabilityDrop acquisition, dangerous gatheringStrength is supported by food and heavy weapon recipes.
DefenceSurvivability and armour tierArmour, shields, charmsSafe exploration and boss accessMining, Swamp, Rival Camp, Troll RidgeDefence should make dangerous resources efficient, not mandatory walls.
RangedSafe damage styleBows, arrows, poison, hidesCombat access, unique dropsMonster farming, bosses, dangerous zonesRanged must depend on Woodcutting + Crafting + Smithing + drops.
HitpointsRisk toleranceFood, armour, positioningPractical access to danger ringsAll combat and remote gatheringHP gates danger naturally through survival, not invisible walls.
